Siri

417 DKK

Introducing the Siri bag with a shiny, reflecting croco silver look featuring an embossed metallic SILFEN lettering on the front. The unique material is perfect for spicing up an everyday look or for a night out! The Siri bag has two interior compartments; one inner zip pocket and a large, open space that closes at the top with a zipper. The bag is easy to clean with a damp cloth. The bag has one handle that is adjustable. The bag can be carried on the shoulder or hang from the arm.
